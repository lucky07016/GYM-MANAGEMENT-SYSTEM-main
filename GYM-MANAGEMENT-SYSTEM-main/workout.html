Split science

PPL spreads volume across push, pull, and legs. Upper/lower fits 4-day weeks. Bro splits add frequency per muscle if you recover fast.

Progression

Add reps or load weekly. If bar speed drops, keep weight and chase crisp form—XP rewards showing up, not ego lifting.

Rest codes

Compounds: 2–3 min. Accessories: 60–90s. Write rest in your head like a countdown HUD.

Pick your split

Loadout notes

Volume dial

Beginners: 10–14 sets per muscle per week. Intermediate+: 14–22 if recovery is green.

Deload week

Every 4–6 weeks, cut sets and loads ~40% for a week—keeps joints and nerves in the game.
